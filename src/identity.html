Webuser identity

Who are webusers, how to track them across several conversations.

What is a webuser

A webuser, often abbreviated as wu is a end-user on Videodesk using a webpage or a mobile app, (s)he is identified by webuser_uid which is a unique hash enabling his (her) identification.

What is a webuser_uid

A webuser_uid is a randomly generated 36 characters long string structured as df9ce149-00e2-6b17-4e20-6f36a2b2b314 (8 hex characters, a dash, 4 hex characters, a dash, 4 hex characters, a dash 12 hex characters. You can fetch your webuser_uid by typing _vdk.getContextParameter('webuser_uid') in the console.

The webuser_uid is created by the front at the beginning of the first visit on a website or on the first app launch. Then, it is stored locally on the device as a string on the mobile or a cookie on the browser. It is used to track visits and visitors for our stats/analytics system.

Only when there has been an interaction with an agent such as a call or a message, this webuser_uid.

Custom data written by the agent, call history and message history is based on the webuser_uid cookie. All the process of transiting data is documented in the Data Flow API page.

Force identity

In some situations, you may wish to force the authentification of a webuser, here is a list of cases:

  • Your website has a private access and you do not want to rely on cookies to identify your users
  • You have a service available on the web and on a mobile app

In both cases you want to insure continuity into the experience of your users across serveral devices (home computer, work computer, smart phone...). So that the agent could access to call history when receiving a call or the conversation history when a text-chat session starts.


_videodesk.webuser_uid = 'your_user_token';
Security warning

This token should not be guessable ! Don't rely on something simple like a name or a customer ID, otherwise a malicious user could steal users' conversation
